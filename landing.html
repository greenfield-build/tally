Does Tally connect to my bank?
No — and that's intentional. Tally never asks for your bank login. You enter bills and income manually (it's faster than you think once you set it up once), or use CSV import to bring in transactions from your bank's download. Many people prefer this because their bank credentials never touch a third party.
Is this really a one-time payment? No subscription?
Really. $29 once. You'll get free updates forever and your cloud sync is included. Your purchase pays for the hosting (which is very cheap on modern infrastructure). The math works because Tally is built by a small studio, not a venture-backed startup that needs to extract recurring revenue from you.
What happens to my data if Tally shuts down?
You can export everything as JSON at any time from Settings → Export. Your data is yours. If the cloud sync ever goes away, the local-storage version of Tally still works in any browser, offline, forever.
Can I use Tally on my phone?
Yes. Tally is a web app that works on phone, tablet, laptop, desktop — anything with a modern browser. Sign in once on each device, your data syncs automatically. No app store, no install required.
